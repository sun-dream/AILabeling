What experience does Balzac have?

Ugo de Balzac, born in 63, has been working in communication since 1989. Gaetano de Balzac, born in 1977, since 1999. Together they have produced a thousand and one works for clients such as Barilla, SantAnna, Italdesign, Pininfarina, Ferrero, Indesit, Zambon, Agnesi, just to name a few. In '20 Bruno arrives, he is a reputation management and media relations expert. That's all, the team is complete.
