      Our professionally trained and certified personnel will thoroughly inspect your property and assess the extent of your fire and smoke damage, allowing us to develop a comprehensive fire and smoke damage plan specific to your needs.     • Smoke and Soot Removal

      Smoke and soot can damage your property and cause health problems for you and your family. We remove all residual smoke and soot in the affected areas.       Fires can compromise the integrity of your structure. We will clean, rebuild, and restore all structural parts, including walls, ceilings, floors, and roofs, to ensure your home is as safe as possible. We will use durable and environmentally friendly materials.

      We understand that your personal or business belongings are important to you. Our specialists will clean and restore salvageable items such as furniture, electronics, documents, and textiles and store them securely.

    • Odor Removal and Deodorization

      If you own a home or office space and suffer from pervasive smoke odors, they can be hard to eliminate. Our deodorization experts can use state-of-the-art techniques to remove odors from your space, leaving it fresh and sanitized. We use environmentally safe products so that you wont be left with harmful chemicals when we are finished with smoke damage restoration.

    • Reconstruction and Renovation

      In other, more serious cases, repairs need to be linked to reconstructions. Zicklin will take care of all your reconstruction and renovation needs. We will perform all repairs and renovation work in a way that returns your property to pre-damage condition or better. We work with you to incorporate whatever design requirements you have.

    • Insurance Claim Assistance

      The insurance claims process can often be confusing. We will help you document your loss, collect all relevant information, and deal directly with your insurance company. We will endeavor to simplify the claims process and ensure you receive maximum compensation for your loss.

    Frequently Asked Questions about Fire Damage Restoration

    My home has just suffered from a damaging fire. What do I do first?

    Get everyone out of harms way and call the emergency services. Once safe, find a fire damage restoration company to assess the damage and make repairs. Document the damage for your insurance claim and notify your insurance company.

    How long does the fire damage restoration process take?

    The length of time for restoration depends upon the severity of the disaster. Restoration of minimum damage takes around a few days, but extensive damage requires weeks or months for the annual restoration.

    Can you remove smoke odors from my property?

    Yes, we use advanced deodorization techniques to effectively remove smoke odors from your property. Dont hesitate to call us for smoke damage restoration. 

    Will I need to move out during the restoration process?

    There may be reasons to leave temporarily and certainly should be if your home isnt structurally sound. We will move you as little as possible during fire damage cleanup but will do so in a way that doesnt cause more difficulty than the situation already entails.

    Do you offer emergency services?

    Absolutely. We are on call 24/7 to respond to your emergency and secure your property.

    How do you handle insurance claims?

    We help you step by step through the insurance claim process, including documenting the damage and providing the information you need in your claim with your insurance company.

    What types of commercial properties do you restore?

    We repair all kinds of retail stores, offices, restaurants, warehouses, etc.

    Is fire damage restoration safe?

    Yes, our team uses the right equipment and follows the right proven safety protocols to ensure a proper restoration process.
