Our Fire Damage Services

Zicklin Contracting offers fire damage restoration services to residences and commercial establishments. Our services include:

Emergency Response

Fires always happen without prior notice, and remedying the damage as quickly as possible is important. By teaming up with an emergency team available 24/7, you will only need to make a phone call, and the response will be immediate. We will close off your property, assess the damages, and commence fire damage repair right away.

Damage Assessment and Inspection

Our professionally trained and certified personnel will thoroughly inspect your property and assess the extent of your fire and smoke damage, allowing us to develop a comprehensive fire and smoke damage plan specific to your needs. In addition, documentation designed to keep you informed and detail your scope of loss will be created when your property is under our care.

Smoke and Soot Removal

Smoke and soot can damage your property and cause health problems for you and your family. We remove all residual smoke and soot in the affected areas. Advanced removal techniques and equipment are used to remove all smoke and soot residues, and the air is tested to ensure that all fine particles are removed for safe air quality.

Structural Cleaning and Restoration

Fires can compromise the integrity of your structure. We will clean, rebuild, and restore all structural parts, including walls, ceilings, floors, and roofs, to ensure your home is as safe as possible. We will use durable and environmentally friendly materials.

Content Cleaning and Restoration

We understand that your personal or business belongings are important to you. Our specialists will clean and restore salvageable items such as furniture, electronics, documents, and textiles and store them securely.

Odor Removal and Deodorization

If you own a home or office space and suffer from pervasive smoke odors, they can be hard to eliminate. Our deodorization experts can use state-of-the-art techniques to remove odors from your space, leaving it fresh and sanitized. Reconstruction and Renovation

In other, more serious cases, repairs need to be linked to reconstructions. Zicklin will take care of all your reconstruction and renovation needs. We will perform all repairs and renovation work in a way that returns your property to pre-damage condition or better. We work with you to incorporate whatever design requirements you have.

Insurance Claim Assistance

The insurance claims process can often be confusing. We will help you document your loss, collect all relevant information, and deal directly with your insurance company. We will endeavor to simplify the claims process and ensure you receive maximum compensation for your loss.
