Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our fire damage restoration services.

Our emergency response team is available 24/7 and will arrive at your property within 30 minutes of your call. We understand the urgency of fire damage situations and prioritize immediate assistance.

Most standard homeowners' and business insurance policies cover fire damage restoration. We work closely with insurance companies to streamline the claims process and ensure that you receive the maximum coverage possible. Our team can help you document the damage and prepare the necessary paperwork for your insurance claim.

The duration of the restoration process depends on the extent of the damage. Minor smoke damage can be restored in a few days, while more extensive damage may take several weeks. Our team will provide you with a detailed timeline after assessing the damage to your property.

We use advanced equipment and techniques to remove smoke and odor from your property. This includes air scrubbers, ozone generators, and thermal fogging machines. Our technicians are trained to identify and treat all surfaces affected by smoke and soot residues, ensuring that your property is thoroughly cleaned and deodorized.

Yes, we offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee on all of our fire damage restoration services. If you are not completely satisfied with the results, we will work with you to make it right. Our goal is to exceed your expectations and restore your property to its pre-damage condition.
